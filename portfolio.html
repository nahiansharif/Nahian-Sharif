Smily University


I have developed a web application that empowers administrators to manage student and teacher data with ease and efficiency. Smily Database


This is a group project at the Universty of Houston-Downtown. The purpose of this Smily Pirates Database is to store information on video games made by Smily Pirates that allows employees to find relevant information based on their roles in an easy way. Users can make changes to the data based on their assigned roles. The Smily Pirates database is a business database that handles all the information about this company and is used by its employees everyday.
