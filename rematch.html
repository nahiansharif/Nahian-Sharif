REmatch


The demo version of this game is available on steam. Link: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2067680/REmatch/



Progression


Player fight the enemies and complete the objective to gain experience (xp), currency, and upgrade points. Leveling up increases player's health.






Skill Tree


Upgrade player gets crowns when they level up. These crowns can be used to upgrade any of your items via skill tree. Your upgraded items get buffed each time you upgrade.






Items


There are many items from weapons to support items. Players have melee weapon and range weapon that allows player to play in their style. Each support item have their own abilities that buffs player's stats for a duration.






Shop


Player earns taka (game currency) by squashing the enemies and completing objectives. Taka can be used in shops to buy support items or crowns.






Save


Player can save their progress when they arrive at the checkpoint. All the saved data will be loaded when player loads up the game from the main menu.






Chess Puzzles


Anyone who never played chess can also solve these puzzles. Objectives shows exactly what player needs to solve. Player just have think and pick the best option.






Detailed Tutorials


Our tutorial section features a user-friendly interface design, complete with descriptive text and video tutorials that provide clear explanations for how each chess piece and puzzles works.
